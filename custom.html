ZHU Custom Enroute Splits

Instructions

  1. Press Define Splits
  2. Click on each sector to assign to a split (1-5).
  3. When finished coloring the splits, press Create Labels.
  4. Click a spot on the map and enter the desired label text.
  5. You can toggle back and forth between defining splits and creating labels.
  6. Use screenshot tool of your choice to share split map.
